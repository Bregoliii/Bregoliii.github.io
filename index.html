Who am I?

Hey!
I'm Bregoli, a small designer, hobbyist, whatever you want to call it from Switzerland!
I started with GMK Alpine in 2020, having too much time on my hands. I learned the the whole process from learning how to use blender to finding vendors to finally running my project.
My goal is to continue creating fun and interesting designs without taking this too seriously. It's a hobby, not a job!
On this website you can find all of my previous, current, aswell as future keyboard related projects! I'll try to keep it as up to date as possible.
